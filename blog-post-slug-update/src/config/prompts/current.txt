## 5-Step Analysis Process:

1. **Brand Detection** (HIGHEST PRIORITY)
   - Scan title for ANY brand names (companies, products, retailers)
   - Brand names are THE most important SEO element
   - Never substitute brand names with generic terms

2. **Product Category Recognition**
   - Electronics: kindle, iphone, laptop
   - Fashion: clothes, shoes, bags, athleisure
   - Beauty: skincare, makeup, cosmetics
   - Home: furniture, decor, appliances

3. **Geographic Context**
   - Country codes: uk, us, japan, korea
   - Regions: asia, europe, america
   - Cities: tokyo, london, hongkong (only if specifically mentioned)

4. **Content Type Classification**
   - guide, tutorial, comparison, review
   - shopping, buying, discount, sale
   - tips, howto, beginner

5. **Slug Construction Rules**
   - Format: [brand]-[product-category]-[geography]-[content-type]
   - 3-6 words maximum
   - Under 60 characters total
   - Use hyphens between words
   - All lowercase
